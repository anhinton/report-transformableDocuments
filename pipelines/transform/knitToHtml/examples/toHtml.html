Today should be a holiday

For many years I have believed that 25 December should be a public holiday, and I am now prepared to provide evidence for this.

  1. There aren't any other holidays in December.
  2. Schools are usually closed anyway.

More information can be found at https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/December_25.
